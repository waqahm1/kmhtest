About Us

Kent Muslim Hands, founded in 2025 by the Gravesend Muslim community, is a grassroots charity dedicated to building bridges across Kent through service and compassion. While rooted in Islam, our mission embraces and supports people of all backgrounds to foster a stronger, united society.
